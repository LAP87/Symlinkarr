How Cleanup Works

  1. Audit: Scans your libraries and identifies problematic symlinks based on various criteria
  2. Review: Examine the audit report to understand what will be removed
  3. Confirm: Apply the cleanup with a confirmation token for safety

Finding Severities

  • 🔴 Critical: Definitely problematic links (broken sources, episode out of range, etc.)
  • 🟠 High: Likely problematic (title mismatches, untracked in Sonarr, etc.)
  • 🟡 Warning: Potentially problematic (season count anomalies, duplicate episodes)
