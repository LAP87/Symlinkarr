How Cleanup Works

  1. Audit: Scan the selected libraries and write a report with candidate findings.
  2. Preview: Open prune preview to review that report and inspect the exact rows that would be removed.
  3. Apply: Paste the report-specific confirmation token only after the preview matches what you expect.

Finding Severities

  • Critical Definitely problematic links, such as broken sources or out-of-range episodes.
  • High Likely problematic links that should be reviewed closely before pruning.
  • Warning Context-only anomalies that may be worth checking, but are not automatic prune targets.
